Case Study 01
SparkEnglish — Google Ads Lead Generation Audit
January – February 2024 • Lead Generation • Google Ads
Pivot Tables CTR vs CVR Device Analysis Campaign Diagnosis

SparkEnglish is a Hong Kong-based online English school running Google Ads to generate free trial sign-ups. I was tasked with auditing two months of campaign performance, diagnosing inefficiencies, and providing actionable recommendations.

January Finding
Finding 01 — Wrong Landing Page Destination
Diagnosis

Brand Awareness accounted for 27% of total budget ($1,092) yet generated zero leads. CTR was 5.5% — the ad was working — but CVR was 0%. The campaign was pointing to the homepage instead of the sign-up page.

Recommendation

Immediately redirect Brand Awareness to the correct sign-up landing page. With existing clicks already coming in, fixing the URL alone was expected to generate leads without additional spend.

February Finding
Finding 02 — Mobile Landing Page Not Converting
Diagnosis

Mobile consumed 47% of total budget ($2,371) while converting at only 2.48% — a 5x gap vs Desktop's 13.23% CVR. Mobile CPL was $1,362 vs Desktop's $195. This device-level inefficiency was the primary driver of rising CPL.

Recommendation

Immediately test the landing page on mobile devices, run Google PageSpeed Insights, and check sign-up form functionality. Once resolved, Mobile CVR was expected to recover toward Desktop levels.

Lumière is a Hong Kong online fashion brand running Google and Meta Ads. I built an interactive Excel dashboard with live KPI cards, slicer filters, and a ROAS-by-campaign breakdown — then diagnosed why overall ROAS was below the 3.0x target.

Key Findings
Budget Inefficiency Across Google & Meta
Google Finding

Search Brand Keywords delivers the highest efficiency — 6% CTR and 5.18x ROAS — yet is underfunded at HK$17,040 vs Non-Brand at HK$28,350. Non-Brand CPC is HK$60 vs Brand's HK$32, signalling targeting that is too broad or too competitive.

Meta Finding

Both Prospecting campaigns are running at 1.10x ROAS — consuming 57.4% of total budget well below the 2.0x breakeven threshold. Retargeting at 5.63x ROAS is significantly underfunded.

Google Recommendation

Pause broad match keywords on Non-Brand and reallocate HK$10,000 to Brand Keywords this week. Expected to improve Google ROAS from 3.58x toward 4.5x within 4 weeks.

Meta Recommendation

Set a 1.5x ROAS floor — if Prospecting falls below that for two consecutive weeks, reduce budget by 20% and reallocate to Retargeting, where 5.63x ROAS signals strong purchase intent.
